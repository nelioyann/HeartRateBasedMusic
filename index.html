HeartBeats (Work In Progress)

Turn your heart rate into music notes

Music has an influence on heart rate. But how does your heart rate determine the music you listen to? HeartBeats turns your heart into an orchestra conductor and your heart rate into music notes. Pair your Mi Band or choose one of the samples down below.
